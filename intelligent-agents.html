Intelligent Agents

// Module: July 2025 · 12 Units · All Completed

The Intelligent Agents module explores the theory and practice of autonomous, goal-directed systems — from foundational agent architectures to cutting-edge deep learning applications. This module bridges classical AI theory with modern real-world deployments.

Covering 12 comprehensive units, the course spans agent-based computing, first-order logic, communication languages (KQML/KIF), natural language processing, adaptive algorithms, and the future of intelligent technologies.
