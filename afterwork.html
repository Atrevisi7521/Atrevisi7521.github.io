Life Beyond Work

As much as I am passionate about my professional journey, I believe in maintaining a balanced life through diverse activities that challenge me physically and mentally. These pursuits complement my professional skills and contribute to my overall well-being and growth.

Dragon Boat Racing

In my free time, I am a proud member of the Dragon Boat team "Latin Dragons" in Singapore. We compete both nationally and internationally, representing Singapore in various competitions across Asia.

Dragon boat racing is not just a sport but a discipline that demands perfect synchronization among all team members. Every stroke must be in unison for maximum efficiency.

This sport teaches me compromise, teamwork, coordination, timing, and mental strength – all qualities that translate directly to effective leadership in my professional life.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

I hold a "Combatives Belt" in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu under the prestigious Gracie Academy. Jiu Jitsu is often referred to as "human chess" because it requires strategic thinking and anticipation of your opponent's moves.

Training regularly in this martial art has taught me to remain calm under pressure and find solutions even when in disadvantageous positions.

This discipline has developed my emotional control, clear thinking during difficult situations, and calm under pressure – skills that prove invaluable in high-stakes business negotiations and problem-solving scenarios.

Boxing

I train as a boxer at "FX Boxing Club" in Singapore. Boxing is one of the most demanding sports physically and mentally, requiring discipline, focus, and perseverance.

The training regimen pushes me beyond my perceived limits and teaches me to maintain precision and technique even when fatigued.

Boxing has instilled in me a sense of resilience, the ability to persevere through fatigue and difficulties, and a commitment to precision that carries over to my professional endeavors.

Volunteer Work

I serve as a personal trainer for a non-profit organization, working with children with special needs at the NCSF Uplift in Singapore. This volunteer work allows me to give back to the community while sharing my knowledge and passion for fitness.

Working with these incredible children has been one of the most rewarding experiences of my life, teaching me patience, empathy, and the value of celebrating small victories.

This volunteer work has enhanced my communication skills, adaptability, patience, and empathy – qualities that make me a more effective leader and team member in my professional roles.

Travel & Cultural Exploration

I have a deep passion for travel and cultural exploration. My most recent journey took me to the beautiful country of Argentina, where I immersed myself in the local culture, cuisine, and landscapes.

During my time there, I learned about Argentina's rich history and traditions, from the passionate tango dance to the complex political history that has shaped the nation.

Travel enriches my cultural awareness, adaptability, language skills, and global perspective – all essential qualities in my role managing international business relationships across diverse markets.
