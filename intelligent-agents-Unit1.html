🤖 Introduction

This first week sets the agenda by looking at the trends in computing that have led to the rise of agent-based computing. We introduce the concept of an agent — what it is and why agents have gained popularity across a breadth of sectors and applications.

🎯 This Week We Shall Learn About
  • The trends in computing that have led to the growth in agent-based technologies
  • The implications of these growing trends and their impact on the wider landscape
  • How agents interact with the environment and each other
  • Examples of agent-based systems and their applications
On Completion of This Week, You Will Be Able To
  • Develop understanding of key concepts of agent-based computing
  • Understand the trends that have facilitated the development of agents as a paradigm
  • Compare different types of agent-based system to contrast their relative merits
// Collaborative Discussion 1 · Units 1–3 · Initial Post
Agent-Based Systems

Dear Colleagues, the main reasons why agent-based systems are rising are to be found in the increased need from organisations to increase their degree of automation when talking about AI implementation — and to decrease human intervention.

As reported by Hughes et al. (2025): "as organizations pursue higher levels of automation, the adoption and integration of AI agents and agentic systems hold considerable potential for business transformation."

The advantages of automation are visible in terms of time saving, process automation, reduced processing time and complex problem solving. "The aim is to maximise the quality and performance of the outcome while minimising the overall costs of the process." (Herrera et al., 2020).

Another important point is cost saving — the most important features of multi-agent systems are cost efficiency, low maintenance costs, reliability, and significant savings when solving complicated tasks (Dorri et al., 2018).

We have discussed the benefits of introducing agent-based systems in organisations. I would love to hear from all of you — can we mention a few other benefits?

— Andrea
