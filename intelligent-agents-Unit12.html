🔮 Introduction

This week's seminar explores the future of deep learning technologies — building on everything learned to date to examine the possible future directions of intelligent technologies. Will advances in deep learning begin to shift towards things once considered the realm of science fiction? Students discuss technologies explored in Week 10 alongside examples from the tutor.

🎯 This Week We Shall Learn About
  • The future directions of intelligent technologies
  • The potential ramifications of these developments
  • How technology will evolve in the coming years
On Completion of This Week, You Will Be Able To
  • Appraise current technologies
  • Assess the potential evolution of technology based on today's examples
  • Evaluate the potential ethical and social considerations
