🔬 Introduction

This final unit focuses on the end-to-end development of knowledge-based systems using ontology — covering development choices, emerging methodologies, and evaluation approaches through a case study. It looks into the future of knowledge-based systems and what to expect from AI applications built on ontological foundations.

🎯 This Week We Shall Learn About
  • Development choices and decisions when building an ontology
  • Emerging methodologies and how they are applied
  • Evaluation approaches for fit-for-purpose knowledge-based systems
On Completion of This Week, You Will Be Able To
  • Critically evaluate methods and approaches to knowledge-based systems
  • Develop the ability to evaluate ontologies for KBS applications
