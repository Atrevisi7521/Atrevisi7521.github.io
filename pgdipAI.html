Postgraduate Diploma in Artificial Intelligence - Machine Learning Module (April 2025)

I am currently pursuing a Postgraduate Diploma in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Essex (UK). This program is enhancing my business expertise with cutting-edge AI knowledge, allowing me to bridge the gap between business strategy and technological innovation.

This module is called "Machine Learning - April 2025" and consists of 12 comprehensive units covering various aspects of machine learning, and their practical applications. Below you can explore my journey through each unit, with detailed notes and reflections on what I've learned.
