Knowledge Representation
and Reasoning

// Module: October 2025 · 12 Units · All Completed

Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (KRR) sits at the philosophical and technical heart of artificial intelligence — exploring how machines can store, organise, and reason over knowledge to make intelligent decisions.

This module spans 12 units covering set theory, logic programming (Prolog), ontology building with Protégé, knowledge elicitation, formalism techniques, and the future of knowledge-based AI systems.
