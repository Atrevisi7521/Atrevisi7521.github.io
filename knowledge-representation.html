Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (KRR) Module Structure

Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (October 2025) is structured around 12 weekly units, each focusing on a specific aspect of Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (KRR) and its applications. The course combines theoretical foundations with practical implementations, preparing students to apply AI solutions in real-world contexts.

Unit 1: Introduction to Knowledge Representation and Reasoning
This unit provides an introduction to knowledge representation and reasoning and introduces common definitions of the terms.
